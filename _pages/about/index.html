I am a researcher in Digital Humanities, currently working as a postdoctoral fellow at the ACCELS Research Centre in Aachen. My work focuses on the application of computational methods (natural language processing, machine learning, artificial intelligence, and network analysis) to the study of complex cultural phenomena.

I serve on the editorial board of Umanistica Digitale and am a member of the Research Committee on Digital Comparative Literature (DCL) within the International Comparative Literature Association (ICLA).

I’m always happy to connect and explore new collaborations, so feel free to reach out!
