## Project Description


Over the past few decades, quantitative approaches have gained increasing relevance in the fields of linguistics and literary studies, offering new insights through data-driven analysis and expanding the methodological toolkit available to researchers. From a didactical perspective, this poses a new challenge, calling for the integration of quantitative methods in courses of study where they could potentially be perceived as being unaligned with the students’ perspective. With the present project, we intend to address this challenge by developing an English-based statistics course —delivered in an independent learning format through a combination of video-based input and self-study activities— specifically designed to account for discipline-specific needs in linguistics and literary studies.
